# Welcome to My AI Journey

If you've followed my work on "The Logic of Politics", you know me as the person who breaks down complex sociological theories, institutional analysis, and comparative politics. I spend my days researching how societies and governments function. So, what am I doing running a blog about AI?

The truth is, **Artificial Intelligence is the biggest technological and societal shift we'll see in our lifetimes**. As a social science writer, I couldn't just stand on the sidelines and watch it happen. I needed to understand it. More importantly, I wanted to explore **how AI can actually help in what I am doing**.

## Building This Website

I am not a software developer. I don't know Python from C++. Yet, here you are, reading this on a custom-built website. 

How? I built this entire blog using **Gemini**.

By conversing with the AI, describing what I wanted, and following its guidance, I managed to create a fully functional, styled, and responsive platform. It was a revelation. It proved to me that AI isn't just a subject of study—it's a powerful tool that lowers the barrier to entry for building and creating.

## What to Expect Here

This blog is my public notebook to document my experiments. I'll be sharing:
- **AI as a Research Assistant:** How can large language models help navigate dense academic literature or organize research notes?
- **AI for Writers:** Testing how these tools can assist in the writing, editing, and communication processes without losing the human voice.
- **My learning process:** The successes, failures, and "aha!" moments of learning to code and build tools alongside AI.

I'm excited to embark on this journey to see how technology can amplify the work of independent scholars and writers. Thanks for being here with me!
